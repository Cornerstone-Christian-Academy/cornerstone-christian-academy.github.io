Our Mission

CCA addresses educational practices from a Christian perspective by offering its students the opportunity to understand themselves and the world around them from a biblical worldview. Some of this education will be formal (chapel, Bible classes/studies, counseling), and some will occur as the faculty and students interact in the normal flow of school activity.

The goal is to facilitate the development of the student spiritually, academically, physically, socially, and emotionally. The school employs administration, faculty, and staff who serve as role models in their Christian walks and who are maturing both professionally and in their Christian faith.

In teaching children to serve the Lord, CCA makes a strong statement for high academic and social standards of conduct. We also believe that school is an extension of the home and partners closely with parents.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in the Bible (both the Old and New Testaments) as the inspired and infallible word of God.

    II Timothy 3:16; II Peter 1:21

  • We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

    Genesis 1:1; John 10:30, 37-38; John 15:26

  • We believe in the virgin birth of Jesus Christ, His vicarious, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and ascension.

    Isaiah 7:14; Matthew 1:23; Luke 1:35; Hebrews 4:15; Hebrews 7:25; I John 2:1; Hebrews 9:12; Colossians 1:14; John 11:25; Acts 1:11; Revelation 19:11-16

  • We believe man was created good and upright, however, man, by voluntary transgression, fell and therefore incurred not only physical death but also spiritual death, which is separation from God.

    Genesis 1; Genesis 2:4-7; Genesis 5:1-2

  • We believe in salvation through the shed blood of Jesus Christ.

    Genesis 3:1-7; Romans 3:19 & 23; John 3:16-19; John 5:24; Ephesians 2:8-10; Titus 3:5-6

  • We believe in water baptism through immersion.

    Mark 16:16; Matthew 28:19-20; Acts 2:41

  • We believe in divine healing through the redemptive work of Christ on the cross.

    Psalms 103:2-3; Isaiah 53:4-5; I Peter 2:24; James 5:14-15

  • We believe in the sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit who enables a believer to live a holy life.

    Ephesians 4:30; Ephesians 5:18; I Corinthians 6:19-20

  • We believe in the Second Coming of Jesus Christ.

    John 5:28-29

  • We believe that the Church is the Body of Christ, the habitation of God through the Spirit, with divine appointments for the fulfillment of the Great Commission.

    Romans 12:4-5; I Corinthians 12:12; Colossians 1:24

  • We believe that divinely called and scripturally ordained ministry has been provided by our Lord for the threefold purpose of:
    Evangelization of the world
    Worship of God
    Building of the body of saints

    Romans 8:9; I Corinthians 12:12-13; Galatians 3:26-28

  • We believe in the millennial reign of Christ with His saints for 1,000 years.

    Isaiah 11:4; Romans 8:17


Non-Discrimination Policy

CCA admits students of any race, color, national or ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs and activities, generally accorded or made available to students at the school.

CCA does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national or ethnic origin in the administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, scholarship and loan programs, athletic or other school-administered programs.


State Approval & Accreditation

CCA has been a member of the Association of Christian Schools and Teachers (ACTS) since 2019.

As of November 2021, CCA is Nationally accredited through ACTS, and the National Council for Private School Accreditation (NCPSA).

CCA is also recognized by the Oklahoma State Department of Education through our membership in the Oklahoma Private School Accreditation Commission (OPSAC).

Oklahoma accredits all nonpublic schools through OPSAC. ACTS and the NCPSA are recognized accrediting agencies for Oklahoma nonpublic schools.
